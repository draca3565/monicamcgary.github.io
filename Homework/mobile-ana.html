Visitor Analysis

The target audience is middle-age male and female worldwide.

The homepage had diferent collections for the visitors to look at clothes for the women and electronics for the men.

It is organized in a linear fashion with side swipes for more options.

Everything was big enough to click on, easy to sort and filter results. No zooming was required to see the text.

Yes, everything was big enough to read, easy to search, and no zoom was required.
