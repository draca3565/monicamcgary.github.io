Weather Planning

Purpose Statement

The purpose for this weather site is to provide accurate current weather. It will have a daily inspirational video. It will also include the city's or town's local events for the week.

Audience Description

The target audience is middle-age male and female ages can range from 18-65. Many of the users will be looking up to see what the weather is like and what kind of activities they can plan or how to dress for the day.

Persona

Pesona: Middle-age Woman

Photo:

  • Fictional Name: Sue Beach
  • Major Responsiblities: Mom, planning activities for her kids and dressing them in appropriate clothing for the day.
  • Demographics: 45 years old, married, and has three kids.
  • Tasks: Finding the weather conditions and preparing her kids accordingly.
  • Environment:Likes to plan fun weekend activites, doesn't have a lot of time to spend on the computer, and likes to know the weather for the day.
  • Quote: "Lets find a weekend adventure!"
